The Book Conservation Diploma at the American Academy of Bookbinding culimnates in a large diploma project. This project requires examples of several different types of work and treatments. Before graduating, I will present this work to be approved by a jury of established book conservators. This is a list of the work I have done specifically for the diploma project.

  • Rebacked cloth binding (in progress)
  • Leather hinge repair (in progress)
  • Paper repair with Japanese tissue (in progress)
  • Paper repair with heat set tissue (in progress)
  • Full conservation binding without leather covering (in progress)
  • Full conservation binding, covered, tooled, with box (in progress)
  • Rebacked leather binding (in progress)
  • Research paper (in progress)
  • Portfolio - electronic, print (in progress)
